Fair Trade Logo Reference Guide

February 20, 2021 - Larren Canapi
Many labels appear on the products you eat and wear, but you should know that all labels are not created equally. Choose authentic products by evaluating products, avoiding bad corporate actors, looking beyond certification, reading labels, and being an activist in this cause.

Fair Trade Fact for the Day

February 21, 2021 - SFU Surge
The Top 4 Challenges in Cocoa Production are:
1. Widespread Poverty in Cocoa Farming Communities
2. Deforestation of Cocoa Trees
3. Gender Inequality
4. Children being forced into labour
Strengthen your knowledge about Fair Trade everyone! And help those in need.

The Coffee Crisis

February 21, 2019 - SFU Fair Trade
Did you know that the coffee industry has a crisis where nearly 61% of producers are selling their coffee at prices under production? Do your part by participating in Fair Trade certified products to help protect these farmers.
